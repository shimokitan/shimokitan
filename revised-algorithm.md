Phase 2: Community & Resonance (Updated)
The opening of the District gates introduces the "Resonance" engine. We prioritize the Artifact over the Influencer.

Weighted Zines: Every user contributes a unique Zine to an Artifact. These are not "likes"; they are documented human responses.

Meritocratic Weight: To defend against bots and paid content, Zines carry "Heat" based on the author’s reputation—earned through successful curation, reporting, and community participation.

Collective Resonance: An Artifact's visibility is the sum of the "Heat" from all attached Zines. High-integrity contributions move an Artifact into the light.

Our Technical Philosophy: The "Anti-Feed"
We believe discovery should be a reward for curiosity, not a result of a dopamine loop.

Featured Logic: For an Artifact to be "Featured," it must cross a Resonance Threshold—a specific level of collective human energy—within a 7-day window. This ensures the District front page represents the community’s current heartbeat.

Cumulative Depth: While "Heat" determines what is featured today, the archive preserves the "Historical Resonance" of every Artifact forever.

Zero-Prediction Policy: We will never "guess" what you want to see. We simply show you what the community has deemed worth keeping.

What We Will Not Do (Revised)
No Engagement-Driven Feeds: We will never use "watch time" or "scroll depth" to rank content.

No Equality for Bad Actors: We will not give the same weight to a bot or a scammer as we do to a verified human curator.

No Invisible Manipulations: Our "Featured" logic is deterministic and based on merit, not a black-box AI.
